The Battle

Breaking the cycle of intergenerational poverty

Why is it that everyone working for us, to make our lives comfortable, is living a life so different from our own? The truth is that even though none of us are intentionally trying to hurt children, we are living in a system that is oppressive in nature.
You might think that because we didn’t build this system, we’re not responsible for it. But we have seen over the years how much community and relatable mentors matter so much to the children. The question is, do you want to inherit this system?
What is important for us to know is that we should not pass on this system to our children.

About the program

A year long foundation program for anyone wanting to tackle the most pressing and complex social challenges faced by children. The program will equip you with the life changing skills you need to create radical change in the world around you!

Are you ready to elect yourself as a leader to embark on a remarkable journey that will not only change the lives of others but also transform your own? At Make a Difference (MAD), you will get the chance to work on a mission to break the cycle of intergenerational poverty by providing quality education and mentorship to children in need of care and protection. It’s your time to stand up for this!

As a volunteer with MAD, you'll have the opportunity to discover your leadership voice, hone your skills, and make meaningful connections with like-minded leaders from across the country. Whether you're a college student looking to gain hands-on experience or a young professional seeking to give back to your community, MAD offers a platform for growth, learning, and personal development.

At MAD, we are trying to gather driven and passionate individuals like you to change the narrative of oppression and poverty for our children. Together we will build a movement that not only helps the underprivileged children survive in this world but also enables them to thrive. Are you ready to join the MADness?
